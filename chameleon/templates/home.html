FutureGrid@Chameleon

FutureGrid@Chameleon is a transitional phase allowing Chameleon users to use our legacy FutureGrid clusters, Alamo and Hotel, configured as they were in FutureGrid: as OpenStack resources. New Chameleon hardware and capabilities will become available next year. Please see our project schedule for more information.

Getting Started

Were you a FutureGrid user? You can activate your Chameleon account and then activate your allocation to continue your work on Chameleon.

Are you a new user? Request an account and then request an allocation or ask the PI of an existing project to add you to their project.

Once you have an account and an allocation, read the user guides and then use the Alamo OpenStack web interface.
