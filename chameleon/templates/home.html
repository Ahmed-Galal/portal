Chameleon is in an initial transition phase. We currently provide virtual environments using OpenStack on our legacy FutureGrid clusters (Alamo and Hotel). We'll provide additional functionality and new hardware in the future! Please see our schedule for more information.

Getting Started

If you are performing research and development on large-scale cloud software and platforms, Chameleon is for you! To get started:

  1. Create an account
  2. Request an allocation.
  3. Once your allocation is approved, follow our OpenStack User Guide

In the near future, we'll also be supporting research on cloud infrastructure.

FutureGrid Users

All active FutureGrid users and projects have been imported into Chameleon. To begin using Chameleon, all you need to do is:

  1. Activate your account
  2. Activate your allocation
